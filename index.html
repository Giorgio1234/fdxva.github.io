The experience

What we provide

Start.

Our headquarters in Memphis, Tennessee (KMEM) are your gateway to world-wide cargo. Custom flight plans, fuel, and routing.

Choice.

Thousands of real-life domestic and international flights; From short hops to long-haul overseas adventures.

airplanemode_active
Fly.

You choose when and where: Fly Caravan or operate the mighty 777. From visual conditions to CAT IIIc Autoland

Feedback.

It's about more than just hours flown; on-time status, departure and arrival weather, fuel, payload, and landing performance.

Our Fleet

From the Mighty 777...
...to the not-so-mighty Caravan!

We fly the same aircraft used by our real life counterpart, including the Boeing 737, 757, 767, 777, MD11, DC-10, ATR-42/72; Airbus A300, A310; and the Cessna Grand Caravan. Members also have the option to use historic aircraft, including the Boeing 747, Boeing 727, Dassault Falcon 20, and Douglas DC-8. Plus an extensive substitution list!

Monthly Events
